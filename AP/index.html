Adrian Pulido

Being Back Home

A Poem for Adrian Pulido

A sunday in Long Island City

Bright Window Light

Yesterday dinner was so warm and sweaty

I loved the little button mushrooms

I love how you bang the wok on the top of the stove

Bang sizzle bang hiss and the sound of my breathing as I watch

Flamin' hot cheetos nachos recipe

There are things to change in the picture below

Like you don't wear those glasses anymore

I would add fish sauce and chilli paste

These small things I know about you
